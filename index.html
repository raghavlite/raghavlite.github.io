Raghuveer Thirukovalluru

Hi, I'm Raghuveer,

I work as a 'Budding Scientist' in the Text and Graph Analytics group at Xerox Research Centre India, Bangalore. I am currently working with Dr. Howard Mizes and Dr. Sriganesh Madhvanath on a Diabetes Risk Stratefication tool. Prior to this work I worked with Dr. Shourya Roy and Mr. Ragunathan Mariappan on Text Summarization and on finding trending topics from a steam of messages.

I graduated with a Bachelors of Technology in Electrical Engineering from IIT Kanpur. At IITK, I've worked on multiple projects in areas of Condition based Monitoring of machines, Chat bots, Word sense Disambiguation with Prof. Nishchal K. Verma and Prof. Harish Karnick.

My areas of interest are Natural Language Processing, Machine Learning and Deep Learning. I am also interested in studying Macroeconomics, specifically the monetary and fiscal policies which drive growth. I'm also a ardent fan of Mikhail Tal and Paul Morphy.
